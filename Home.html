IT Systems & Devices

What do you actually learn in the study IT Systems & Devices?

In the direction of IT Systems & Devices, you learn basic IT skills, covering working with virtualization, networks, cloud and Windows and Linux servers. In addition, you learn in projects how to integrate IT systems and audiovisual equipment with each other. There are also three main topics on the agenda: Technology in the audiovisual world: you will learn everything about mobile management at events and working with multi-projection (creating a good technical image via beamers, multiple screens and 3D video mapping). Live broadcast: here you learn to set up an error-free live stream for different devices, such as mobile and computer. You will also get to work with virtual studios and augmented reality. System Integration: Integrating audio and video with Wi-Fi, access control, security, projection and communication systems. Here you will learn to make a 'total solution' for companies, houses or, for example, large pleasure yachts.
